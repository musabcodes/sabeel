About Sabeel

Giving sadaqah to your local masjid shouldn't require a cash box in the lobby or knowing someone on the board. Sabeel is a centralized platform that lets you find and support the organizations in your area — directly from your phone, on your own time, at your own pace.

How it works

Sadaqah without the friction

  • Find what's near you

    See every masjid and Muslim organization in your area in one place — no searching, no guessing, no word of mouth.

  • Give from anywhere

    Support your local organizations digitally — set up one-time or recurring sadaqah without ever needing to be there in person.

  • All in one place

    Track your giving, follow the organizations you care about, and stay connected to your community — all from a single dashboard.
