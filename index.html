Support the places that hold your community together.

Sabeel makes it easy to find, connect with, and give to your local masajid and Muslim community organizations.

Many of us want to support our local masajid and community organizations but aren't always sure where to start — or how to stay consistent. Sabeel brings together the tools and information you need to contribute meaningfully to the places closest to you.

  • Discover masajid and Muslim organizations near you — with the information that actually matters.
  • Give directly and consistently with simple, modern tools — no friction, no middlemen.
  • Stay connected to the communities and causes you care about — all in one place.
